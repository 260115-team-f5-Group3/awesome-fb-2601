## Vim mode

- Normal mode: Press esc any mode(default)
- Insert mode: Press i
- Visual mode: Press v
- Commandline mode: Press : on Normal
    - :{num} go to num th line
    - :q quit
    - :W write
    - :q! overide and quit(Unsave)
    - :wq write and quiit
